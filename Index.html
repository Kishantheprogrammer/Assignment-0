Let's Learn How To Make Eggo Waffles!

To begin, you want to grab yourself a pack of Eggo waffles. Then you want to either turn the toaster on, preheat the pan, or grab a plate to warm it up in the microwave. Once your pan or toaster is preheated, place the waffles in the pan or toaster and let cook for 3 - 5 minutes. If your using the microwave, keep cooking within 30 secs until liking. Lastly, put some syrup ontop and enjoy!
